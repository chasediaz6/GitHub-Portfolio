About

I first became interested in data analytics through my passion for baseball. As a collegiate player, I recognized that statistics such as batting average do not effectively convey a player's ability, and using data such as spray charts can help a team strategize to win games. As I completed my Bachelor's coursework at FSU, I took a Big Data course that opened my eyes to the endless possibilities of machine learning. My passion for sports and interest in analytics led me to join the FSU Sports Analytics Club, where I was exposed to data science technologies and applications in sports. These experiences inspired me to pursue a career in data science.
